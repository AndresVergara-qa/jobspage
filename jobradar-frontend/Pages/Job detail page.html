Job Description

CloudScale Solutions is looking for an experienced Senior QA Engineer to join our core infrastructure team. In this role, you will be responsible for defining, implementing, and maintaining our automated testing frameworks across our entire distributed system. You will work closely with developers to ensure that quality is built into every stage of the software development lifecycle.

Our platform handles millions of concurrent requests, requiring a meticulous approach to performance testing and reliability engineering. You’ll be the champion of quality, helping us scale our services while maintaining the high availability our enterprise clients expect.

Key Responsibilities

  • check_circle Design and implement robust automated test suites for web applications and backend APIs.
  • check_circle Integrate automated tests into CI/CD pipelines to ensure continuous quality delivery.
  • check_circle Identify, document, and track software defects using industry-standard tools.
  • check_circle Collaborate with product managers and developers to define clear acceptance criteria.

Requirements

  • arrow_forward 5+ years of experience in software quality assurance with a focus on automation.
  • arrow_forward Proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript and frameworks like Playwright, Cypress, or Selenium.
  • arrow_forward Experience testing RESTful APIs and distributed systems.
  • arrow_forward Strong understanding of SQL and NoSQL databases.
  • arrow_forward Familiarity with containerization (Docker) and Kubernetes is a plus.
